## Concepts

Topological Equivalence

$$ 
\begin{eqnarray*} 

\LARGE
X \simeq Y \iff \exists_{\textstyle{homeomorphism} \varphi:X \rightarrow Y}
\end{eqnarray*} 
$$ 
{: style="text-align: left"}

Homotopy

\begin{eqnarray*} 

\LARGE
X \simeq Y \iff \exists_{\textstyle{homeomorphism} \varphi:X \rightarrow Y}
\end{eqnarray*} 
$$
